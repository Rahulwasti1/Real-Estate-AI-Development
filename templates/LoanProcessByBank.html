"Home Loan Application Process: A Step-by-Step Guide"

"Many banks and financial institutions follow a structured process for lending , often represented as a flowchart. This flowchart provides a clear, step-by-step overview of the lending procedures typically used by these institutions. Below, you'll find a detailed guide illustrating each stage of the loan process, from application to approval , giving you a better understanding of how the loan journey works."

Loan application from the prospective clients obtained at branch
↓
Verifying the loan application and collection of required documents / information
↓
Conducting personal interview with the client
↓
Obtaining credit information about prospective client
↓
Conducting site visit
↓
Valuation of proposed collateral will be carried by the registered Valuator of the Bank
↓
Credit Appraisal (CA) prepared by branch
↓
CA forwarded at the attention of Credit Risk Assessment Department (CRAD) along with the documents at Head Office for approval.
↓
CRAD, after assessing the risks and having addressed the same, moves the file to approving authorities and receives the same after the decision.
↓
Upon approval of CA, The complete credit file will be sent by CRAD to Credit Administration Department (CAD)
↓
    CAD will:
  1. Review the credit proposal and note the approval comments.
  2. Inform the Branch about the approval.
  3. Check the requirement of documents (Checklist) vis-a-vis the received ones and note the incomplete documents.
  4. Prepare the Credit Facility Offer Letter and other security documents.
  5. Send (1) the list of incomplete documents, (2) security documents, and (3) the original of the approval (keeping a copy) to the Branch.
↓
    Branch will:
  1. Inform the customer about the approval and further requirements based on the List of incomplete documents and security documents provided by CAD.
  2. Make the documents complete and execute the security documents properly.
  3. Send scanned copy of the documents to CAD for loan disbursement./rate limit update.
  4. Keep the original executed security documents with it in fire proof cabinet.
↓
    CAD will:
  1. Review the documents including the security documents. In case of any confusion, consult Legal Department.
  2. Check documents vis-à-vis the checklist.
  3. Disburse the loan confirming that all the documents are complete.

With these clear steps, the journey to securing your home loan becomes much more straightforward. Each stage, from application to loan disbursement, is designed to support you in reaching your homeownership goals. Now that you’re familiar with the process, you can move forward confidently.
